About Me.

Hi, I’m Lola. I’m a web designer and developer working towards carving a niche and expanding my horizon in the Frond-End Web Development Tech space.

I am originally from Ekiti State, Nigeria. I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria.
Growing up in the Lagos State was a bit challenging for me, but it shaped to be smart and hardworking. I also learnt to find an opportunity in every situation and the importance of being diverse.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Art & Education in African Language (Yoruba) from the Universitry of Lagos Nigeria, which is not in any way related to computer programming.

I found the love for web designing and developing in my second second semester during my masters, when i took an elective course Management of Information System, and i had to build a website as my project. I found so much interest in web development after this project, because i learnt a lot about coding while building the website and this made me want to keep knowing more.

Currently, I'm a master's student of International Tourism Development at the Deggendorf Institue of Technology, with specialisation in tourism management, customer experience management, tourism research, smart tourism and management of information system. I make research in the tourism market and i also love to build and design websites. I am also currently enrolled at the ReDI School NRW, learning to become a Frontend Web developer, i am currently in HTML/CSS class, and i will also be taking classses in JavaScriot and React to perfect my coding skills.
